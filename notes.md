## Stocks

- Ownership of a percentage of a company
- Payoff is linear to the company's profit

## Options

- Call option: right (not obligation) to buy a stock at strike price K before expiry.
- Strike price: fixed price specified in the contract.
- Call payoff at expiry: max(S_T - K, 0) where S_T, S subscript T, is stock price at expiry.
